The Researchers Valley

We are a community of tech-minded people gathered around to make the world a better place, we want to achieve this goal in several ways.
Our mission is to gather tech-minded people around the world, work with them, improve them and ourselves and create useful stuff for the world; the first service that we created was this website, it was created so that tech-minded folk or researchers can gather around, share their knowledge and build things that can have positive impact to at least one person's life.

What the community is really about?

Here are a list of things that we are focused on:

  • Creating something good for the world, people, your team or anyone, just something that helps people is OK
  • CTF & Hacking Competitions to upgrade everyone's skill-set
  • Programming Competitions
  • Creating a community of like-minded people
  • Contributing for the good of people and everyone around you

How to join?

As an individual if you are into one of the following, you can join us:

  • Playing on a CTF
  • Played on CTFs
  • Learning a programming language
  • Reading a book about subjects related to IT
  • Researching subjects related to IT
  • Creating or created something to help people (even if it's 20 lines of code)
  • Getting their feet wet in bug bounties
  • Pursuing a degree & interested in tech
  • Just interested in tech

However, it is required to understand that our intentions and intentions of those joining us can match, these intentions are about helping and improving the world.
Our community remains white-hat & neutral, we have nothing to do with attacking people or performing any type of malicious activity, when it comes down to politics, race, culture and religion, our community remains neutral.

If you want to become a member you can join our Discord channel, there you can start a project or talk about the things that you want to share with a bigger audience and we will give you the 'Invitation Code' which is required for signup, after that you can join as a member.
