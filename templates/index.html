Who are we

We are a community of tech-minded people gathered around to make the world a better place, we want to achieve this goal in several ways.
Our mission is to gather tech-minded people around the world, work with them, improve them and ourselves and create useful stuff for the world; the first service that we created was this website, it was created so that tech-minded folk or researchers can gather around, share their knowledge and build things that can have positive impact to at least one person's life.
